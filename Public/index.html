About Dr. Harrison

Dr. Michael Harrison is a board-certified cardiologist with over 15 years of experience in diagnosing, treating, and preventing cardiovascular diseases. His patient-centered approach combines cutting-edge medical technology with personalized care.

After graduating with honors from Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine, Dr. Harrison completed his residency at Massachusetts General Hospital and fellowship in interventional cardiology at the Cleveland Clinic.

  • Fellow of the American College of Cardiology
  • Published 50+ Research Papers
